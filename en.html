First time in Atyrau

First time ever in Atyrau, you are welcome to Anti-Office coworking place, which designed as “loft” style, modern and relaxed working area. We believe, you will find it interesting especially if you are freelancer, translator, programmer, art designer or if you starting a new business. Coworking is not only dedicated for individuals as isolated work area, but it also excellent networking opportunities with other business minded people around. It is not just renting a seat, but its access to all sort of master classes, trainings, consultations and discussions. It is the place where new ideas born, develop and excelled. For that, we have tried to make comfortable place, available libraries and conference rooms, unlimited tea/coffee and cookies.

Our Advantages

Cost saving

We sure you will find the best suitable rates among offered list, starting from hourly payment to monthly subscriptions with all its benefits included. If offered rates are not suitable for your business, please let us know, we will make it happen.

Effectiveness

This is not only renting “just a seat”, this is excellent opportunity to be introduced with other creative business initiators, settling networking, by communication and offering effective business deals.

Functionality

Anti-Office will periodically organize different type of master-classes, trainings and consultations, inviting influential business partners and individuals to share their stories and business cases.
